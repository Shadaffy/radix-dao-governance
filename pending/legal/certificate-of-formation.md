## Article I — Name

The name of the Limited Liability Company is:

**Radix DLT DAO LLC**

---

## Article II — Entity Type

The Company is organized as a **Non-Profit Decentralized Autonomous Organization Limited Liability Company** under:

* the Marshall Islands Limited Liability Company Act (52 MIRC Ch. 3)
* the Marshall Islands DAO Act (52 MIRC Ch. 7)
* the Marshall Islands Non-Profit Entities Act (2020)

---

## Article III — Purpose

The Company is organized exclusively for charitable and educational purposes, including:

* Education in decentralized ledger technology and its applications
* Advancement of cryptographic systems and open-source software protocols
* Research and governance of distributed and decentralized networks
* Support for token-based participation and distributed governance mechanisms

No part of the net earnings of the Company shall inure to the benefit of, or be distributable to, any Member, Delegate, or other individual, except as reasonable compensation for services rendered or reimbursement of legitimate expenses incurred.

## Article V — Management

The Company is managed by its Members, as further described in the Operating Agreement. Management functions are delegated to elected Members (Delegates) as set forth in the Operating Agreement.

The Company has no managers, directors, officers, or trustees within the meaning of traditional corporate law. Authority rests with the Members collectively, exercised through on-chain governance as described in the Operating Agreement.

---

## Article VI — Duration

The Company has perpetual duration unless dissolved in accordance with the Operating Agreement or by operation of law.

## Article VIII — Member Rights Notice

> **Notice required by Marshall Islands DAO Act:**
> The rights of members of this Company may differ materially from the rights of members in other limited liability companies. The Operating Agreement may provide that fiduciary duties owed between members, and between members and the Company, may be defined, reduced, or eliminated. Transfer restrictions on membership interests may apply. Prospective members are advised to review the Operating Agreement carefully before participating in governance.

---

## Article IX — Non-Distribution Statement

The Company is organized on a non-distribution basis. Upon dissolution, remaining assets must be transferred to an entity meeting the qualifying criteria set out in the Operating Agreement §9.2. No assets may be distributed to Members or Delegates upon dissolution.
